Warping Game

This personal project is a collection of various games that link to each other, and have a rather strange continuous narrative. It is inspired by my dream journal- specifically the way I think and what I feel while in long-lasting dreams. The games use Unity/C#, PuzzleScript, Flickgame, Lua/PICO-8, and Godot. I've been working on it for a while, and have recently uploaded the final game. I submitted it to two college game showcases (at RIT and RPI), and it was a finalist at both of them.
